Welcome!

This is an online chessboard. You can play with your friends or you can battle a chess engine called Flux Chess.

To get started click this link to register. If you are alredy registered login here.

After registering or logging in you can start a new game. You can play against other registered persons or a computer which is running a chess engine. If you want to play against a person, the person must be registered too and you also need to know the email address. New moves from your opponent will be send to you by email.

Good luck!
